Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between subscriptions and lifetime licenses?

Subscriptions give you ongoing access to RinaWarp with automatic updates. Lifetime licenses are a one-time purchase that gives you permanent access to all Pro features forever, including all future updates.

Can I upgrade from Starter to Creator or Pro?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time. Your new features will be activated immediately. Contact support@rinawarptech.com if you need help with the upgrade process.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes. If you're not satisfied within 30 days of purchase, contact support for a full refund. No questions asked.

What's included in "Pro access"?

Pro access includes production deployment actions, high-impact tools (with confirmation), and power-user workflows. This is the full RinaWarp experience for serious system management.

Can I switch from monthly to lifetime?

Absolutely. Contact sales@rinawarptech.com and we'll help you transition to a lifetime license with credit for your previous payments.

Do you offer team or enterprise pricing?

Yes! Contact sales@rinawarptech.com for team licenses and custom pricing for larger organizations.
